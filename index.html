We are a co-working space with offices and private suites for aspiring entrepreneurs and freelancers to grow and succeed.

About Us

We are a community of developers, graphic designers, salespeople, consultants, lawyers, accountants and appraiser that want to create a welcoming environment for startups to thrive in. We are new in the community, being established in August 2018. Located at Ave. Munoz Rivera, we work closely with students from the University of Puerto Rico and the community of Rio Piedras to provide the facilities needed to grow successful businesses and pursue their endeavours.

Facilities and Perks

Our community enjoys many resources that we provide them with:

  • Fully Furnished
  • Conference Rooms
  • Corporate Address
  • Mailing Service
  • Fiber Optic Internet
  • Parking
  • Coffee
  • Copiers

Prices

Prices to join our community depend how big your team is and what spaces you would all want to use:

  1. Single desk
  2. Double desk
  3. Table space
  4. Office space

All teams have access the conference room for meetings and such.
